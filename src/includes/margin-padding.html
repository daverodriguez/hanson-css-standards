Padding/Margin Utility

Padding and margin utility classes are typically used for one-off items that dont necessarily require a module definition. In the past we have used a shorter syntax for these (.mtl, .pas, etc), but we have clarified the naming of the padding and margin styles. While a bit longer, they are more readable/understandable. These should only be used as needed and are not a replacement for creating modules. The styles are generated from a combination of sass maps located in 2 locations. Sass maps that are solely associated with the padding and margins utility are in scss > utilities > utility > _padding-margin.scss. Global sass maps that are used to create this utility such as "breakpoints" are in scss > utilities > _utility-variables.scss

The examples below show padding only. Just replace the word "padding" with "margin" to use either one
